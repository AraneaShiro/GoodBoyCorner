    4/Database schematic

        User:
            -Id
            -Login
            -Password
            -NbToken

        User can create,edit,remove quests and rewards.
        They can buy reward with tokens.
        Claimed reward(When the action will be done IRL).
        They must be logged in to go on the website.
        They can signout.


        Quest:
            -Title (Max 16char)
            -Description
            -Prize(token or reward)
            -Status (waiting,accepted,finished)
            -Expiration day
            -CreationDay

        Quest can be created,edited(by the creator only if its not accepted or finished), remove(by the creator only if its not accepted or finished).
        They can be accepted by another user.It will remove it from the waiting list.
        They can be finished by another user.It will give the prize(reward or token) and go in the finished list of the one that finished it.

        Rewards:
            -Title(Max 16char)
            -Description
            -Status (claimed or waitingToBeClaimed)
            -Price (int)

        Rewards are created by users.
        They can be on their own in the shop, where they can be bought for a number of token.
        Or be associated to a Quest and so wont have a price.
        Reward can be possesed by a user and claimed by one.
